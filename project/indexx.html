My Addiction

This website is dedicated to my addiction of anime. Anime is a japanese animated cartoon and the source material is usually either manga (comic) or a light novel. I started watching anime when i was around seventeen years old, I met a group of friends at my workplace and they showed me my very first anime "Samurai Champloo."


Ever since then I've explored many different storylines and adventures and I gradually became quite the otaku, which is the japanese word for nerd or geek. Thus, I have named this site "Impromptu Otaku" because I never really planned on becoming so fascinated by the stories I read or watch, but I'm happy I have.
